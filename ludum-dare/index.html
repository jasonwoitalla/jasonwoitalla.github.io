Ludum Dare

Game Jam Competitoin

Ludum Dare is a game jam competition. This means I need to create a fully-featured game that fits a given theme and submit it by a certain deadline. For Ludum Dare, I have to submit my entries within 48 hours of receiving the theme. To date, I have competed in 5 of these.

Ludum Dare 40: Blob Story: Cave Rescue!

Submitted: December 2017

  • Theme: The More you Have, the Worse it is
  • Language: JavaScript / enchant.js

Blob Story: Cave Rescue! is a 2D exploration game with a randomly generated map. The player has to navigate the random cave to find “blobs” hidden throughout. As they collect more blobs the player gets slower and the rooms get darker. The player must navigate back to the main room to return the collected “blobs.” They must avoid enemies on their travels.

Ludum Dare 38: Lava Moon

Submitted: April 2017

  • Theme: A Small World
  • Langauge: JavaScript / enchant.js

Lava Moon is a 2D platformer. The player has the entire bottom of the screen to move around. By jumping, they can shift the effect of gravity to use the entire top of the screen. The goal is to survive waves of enemies and powerup your character.

Ludum Dare 37: Dungeon Train

Submitted: December 2016

  • Theme: One Room
  • Language: JavaScript

Dungeon Train is a top-down simulator game. The idea is that the player has one room where they can manage life. They have farmers and soldiers. The player has to properly balance the amount of food while keeping their room safe.
